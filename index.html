Teamwork - Immersion - Fun

Task Force Valkyrie is an Arma unit that was founded in August 2021. Unlike other milsim units, Task Force Valkyrie is focused on smaller, special forces style operations with a tight-knit community.
Over the last two years, we have battled our way across twelve campaigns that have taken us from the far future worlds of Titanfall, to the beaches of Normandy and everything inbetween.

Unfortunately, this unit is private and unless you have been sent here by a member, you won't be able to join. This website was more intended to play around with web design stuff.
However, feel free to have a look around, check out our latest campaign, gallery and operation index.
