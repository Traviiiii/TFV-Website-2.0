LEGENDS OF THE UNDERGROUND

Campaign 15 takes us back to the second World War. This time we're focusing on the stories of the resistance and how everyday people helped the fight against the German war machine.
From propaganda to sabotage, those who defied the nazis went underground to continue the fight, even when all hope seemed lost...


Fight like a Guerilla

Those who resisted Nazi occupation weren't always able to access large amounts of ammunition or firearms. They often had to make do with what they could find.

LOTU will challenge players to scavenge and take advantage of the battlefield to secure better weaponry and keep it topped up. Civilians in hiding may also be able to offer players valuable intel which can change the tide of the battle.

A World at War

The main campaign for Legends of the Underground will feature 3 distinct fronts, each with their own fighting style and environment that you will need to master.

Take part in the historic battle of Stalingrad and the race to Berlin as the USSR. Then you will enter the Polish resistance and partake in the Warsaw Uprising. Finally you will join the French Resistance in their mission to retake Paris.

The Fight Continues...

Outside of regular operations, the persistent modes you have grown to love over the course of Dawnbreaker make a return, all with a World War 2 themed coat of paint and numerous QoL updates.

Sabotage, Clash, Team Deathmatch, Sandbox, Liberation will all make a return. In addition to these, mission replays of LOTU missions are available in addition to a select number of missions from Who Dares Wins.
